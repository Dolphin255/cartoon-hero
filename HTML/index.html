About Po, Kung Fu Panda

Po might just be the laziest, clumsiest panda in the Valley of Peace, but he secretly dreams of becoming a kung fu legend.

Po and his friends

Characteristics

  1. His favorites are food and kung fu.
  2. He is usually easygoing, playful, and cocky like a big kid
  3. He has an infinitely strong sense of justice, willing to risk his life to protect others.
  4. He can be quite immature and oblivious in some situations.

Occupation

  • Noodle-maker (formerly)
  • Dragon Warrior
  • Kung fu master/instructor
